About the Heaps

Binary Heap

An array-based tree structure. O(log V) for both insert and extract-min operations. In practice, this is very fast for sparse graphs due to memory cache locality.

Binomial Heap

A collection of binomial trees. Excellent for merging structures. Operations are typically O(log V).

Fibonacci Heap

A lazy structure that defers work. O(1) amortized time for `insert` and `decrease-key`. O(log V) for `extract-min`. Theoretically the best for dense graphs where `decrease-key` operations vastly outnumber `extract-min` operations.
